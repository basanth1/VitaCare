Virtual Hospital is a comprehensive healthcare app that allows users to seamlessly book appointments with doctors, upload prescription images for automatic medicine recognition, and order prescribed medicines directly through an integrated pharmacy system. The app leverages AI to extract details from uploaded prescriptions, helping users understand their medications and simplifying the ordering process. By combining teleconsultation, intelligent prescription analysis, and online medicine delivery, Virtual Hospital offers a convenient, all-in-one solution for managing healthcare from the comfort of home.
